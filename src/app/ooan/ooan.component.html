ERMANNO SCERVINO 2022
The Brief
Born in 2000 in Florence with the mission of making women feel beautiful in their own skin and dress, Ermanno Scervino has earned its own space in the contemporary fashion scene. During the years, the brand introduced interesting concepts such as “couture à porter” by creating every product with the best possible quality and craftmanship. And also elevating utilitarian products as the padded jacket to high fashion. Although still consistent with the original strategy, the brand needs an ultimate upgrade to communicate with the well-defined target profiles to keep a stronger positioning in the desired market segment – One-of- a-Kind luxury.

What We Did
According to the Golden Thread research, there is a need to connect the primary target profile – the Mastermind, to the aspired market segment – One-Of-A-Kind Luxury through the Point of Differentiation – to Treat. Perapolis and Ermanno Scervino agreed to step up the brand reputation through the introduction to the world of bespoke garments to clients through the adoption of the concept of ‘Couture-à-Porter’ - haute couture elements for every special moment in a woman's life. The desired starting point for the brand was to leverage on the high quality and craftmanship, together with the heritage and Italian know-how inherited through the years, which are the client’s reasons to purchase. The strategy revolves around the launch of a capsule collection of eveningwear, called One-Of-A-Night, to be purchased either online in the brand’s e-commerce or in the main boutiques in Europe and Miami (US). There, the capsule will have an exclusive corner where clients can also take advantage of sartorial corrections and personalization to the capsule clothing. The communication for the promotion of the new launch features a campaign where two young women will go in search of the meaning of beauty and passion. The ad is expected to increase the focus on meaning and knowledge that the profile of the Mastermind seeks.
