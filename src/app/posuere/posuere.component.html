AVAVAV 2022
The Brief
AVAVAV is a young brand based in Florence that is driven by innovative design and ethical manufacturing. Beate Karlsson, the new creative director, had also completely reinvented the brand. During the recent rebranding and attempts to adapt to the current trends, the brand lost its target persona and a clear direction for where it should go next. As a result, significant sales losses occurred, the original target market was unable to adopt the new product, and no new customers were identified.

What We Did
According to the Golden Thread research, there is a need to reconnect with the primary target profile - the Communal Wearer. Perapolis and AVAVAV agreed to launch a marketing campaign introducing the AV Tribe and their everyday use of the brand's iconic product, driven by a mission and community spirit. The Perapolis strategy for AVAVAV is to also unveil a new logo in three phases in order to make a greater effect in the Brand Luxury market. The logo is inspired by the classic AVAVAV monogram and showcases its merchandising characteristics. To market the logo, we used a three-month approach that included a once-a-month drop of a new item: sunglasses, a bucket hat, and a new AVAVAV bag. The primary purpose is to highlight the brand's presence in its own market category while capitalising on the "To Recognize" point of differentiation. The brand is adopting a community-focused advertising method, the debut of the first product alongside the campaign involved a pop-up store in Florence to start with. Pop-ups provide AVAVAV with the physical space required to hold events that generate buzz and establish a strong community around the values and cult products of the brand.
